About Us

Empowering seamless communication through advanced deep learning. ECENYAS bridges gaps by translating Filipino and American Sign Language into text, and by converting spoken language into signs. Our comprehensive support includes major Philippine languages: Bicolano, Kapampangan, Cebuano, Hiligaynon, Ilocano, and Waray.

Our Mission

To empower the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing community by providing accessible, real-time, and accurate communication tools. We strive to bridge the gap between spoken language and sign language through advanced AI technology.

Our Vision

A world where communication has no barriers. We envision a society where technology seamlessly integrates sign language into daily interactions, fostering inclusivity and understanding for everyone.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Pushing the boundaries of technology to create smart, adaptive solutions for complex communication challenges.

Inclusivity

Designing for everyone. We believe accessibility is a right, not a privilege, and we build with that in mind.

Integrity

We are committed to data privacy, ethical AI development, and serving our community with transparency.
