Welcome to ECENYAS

Bridging communities, one sign at a time.

Empowering seamless communication through advanced deep learning. ECENYAS bridges gaps by translating Filipino and American Sign Language into text, and by converting spoken language into signs. Our comprehensive support includes major Philippine languages: Bicolano, Kapampangan, Cebuano, Hiligaynon, Ilocano, and Waray.
