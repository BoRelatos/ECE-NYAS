How to Use

  1. 1 Select your sign language mode (FSL for Filipino Sign Language or ASL for American Sign Language)
  2. 2 Click "Start Camera" to enable your webcam
  3. 3 Position yourself clearly in front of the camera and perform sign language gestures
  4. 4 Click "Refresh" to clear all outputs and start a new translation session
